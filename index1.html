About Axial Woodworking

Founded with a passion for wood and honest craftsmanship, Axial Woodworking merges modern engineering with old-world joinery. Each kitchen we create is a statement — tailored to your space, habits, and aesthetic.

From solid wood countertops to dovetailed drawers, we work with domestic hardwoods and sustainable materials. Based in the heart of the design district, we serve homeowners, architects, and builders who demand excellence.
